Welcome to Knot It!

Create A Knot From The List

  • Over Hand Knot: Safety knot
  • Square Knot: Joins the end of two ropes under tension
  • Girth Hitch: Attaches webbing or cord to an anchor or harness
  • Figure Eight Bend: To join the ends of two ropes
  • Water Knot: Joins the ends of tubular webbing
  • Bowline: To anchor a rope
  • Triple Bowline: To anchor a bite of rope(not the end of the rope)
  • Clove Hitch: Anchors the end of rope under tension
  • Clove Hitch Middle of Rope: Forms and anchor in the middle of rope
  • Double Figure Eight: Forms a fixed loop in rope
  • Figure Eight Slip Knot: Creates an adjustable bite in rope
  • Middle of the Rope Prusik: Moveable friction hitch
  • End of the Rope Prusik: Moveable friction hitch at the end of a rope
  • Rerouted Figure of Eight: To tie into a climbing rope
  • Munter Hitch: Forms a mechanical belay
  • Super Munter Hitch: Creates a high friction lower
  • Auto Block: Moveable friction hitch with rope or webbing, releaseable
  • Munter Mule Knot: Creates a releasable anchor knot
